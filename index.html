Game Description

  • Legends Match takes match-3 puzzles to the next level! Embark on a thrilling maze adventure filled with challenging puzzles, strategic decisions, and legendary companions from fairy tales. Your goal?

FAQ

  • Is Legends Match free to play?
    Yes! The game is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases.
  • How does the maze system work?
    Each level presents a different maze where you must solve match-3 puzzles to unlock the correct paths and escape.
  • Can I choose my fairy tale companions?
    Yes! You can unlock and select different companions, each with unique skills to help you.
  • What are random skills?
    These are special abilities that appear during the game, adding a new layer of strategy to each match.
  • Will there be new updates?
    Absolutely! New mazes, characters, and challenges will be introduced regularly.
  • Is there a competitive mode?
    Yes! You can compare your escape time with global players on the leaderboard.
